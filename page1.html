The Gods and Goddesses of Mount Olympus

and some facts about them

Zeus

  • Considered king of gods.
  • God of Sky, Lightning, and Thunder.
  • The eagle, thunderbolt, bull and oak are all symbols associated with him.

Poseidon

  • God of the Sea
  • Believed to be the creator of horses. According to the stories, Demeter asked Poseidon to create the world's most beautiful animal he did just that and created horses.
  • A major symbol associated with Poseidon is the Trident. He is holding the trident in almost every depiction of him from books to movies to sculptures

Hera

  • Protector of women. She presided over marriages and birthsa
  • Has a festival dedicated to her. This was an all-female athletic competition which was held in some city-states, called Heria.
  • Hera had quite a few different names and some of them included: Alexandros, Hyperkheiria and Teleia.
